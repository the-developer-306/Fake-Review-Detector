Welcome to Fake Review Detector

The Fake Review Detector uses advanced machine learning models to determine if a review is genuine or fake. It analyzes review content, rating, and helpful votes to make an accurate prediction.

How It Works

The system processes the review text and related data to identify if it's a genuine review or a fabricated one. It considers the sentiment, review length, and other features to classify the review accurately.

What Inputs Are Needed?

  • Review Text: The content of the review.
  • Overall Rating: The star rating given by the reviewer (1-5).
  • Helpful Votes Ratio: The ratio of helpful votes received for the review.
